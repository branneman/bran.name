How to setup private binds in Ventrilo

Published 2008-12-11

» Press the arrow -> next to the Bindings

» Press 'New', and enter a name for this binding (this is some kind of profile name, you can have multiple, but have to select one for now)

» Click Add

» Press the Hot Key you want to use when talking in your private bind, this must be different from your normal Hot Key

» Click PTT advanced

» Click PTT advanced, click ok

» Click User-2-User

» Enter the nicknames of the ones you want to hear what you say, comma-seperated, without spaces in between. This is case sensitive!

» Click ok twice, and select your newly created bind in the Bindings dropdown. if you dont have it selected, it wont work
